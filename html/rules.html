RULES

Welcome to the Escape Room. Here are some rules to help you get started!

  1. Follow instructions for every challenge: Every challenge has a tutorial to help you using it.
  2. Time limit: There's a time limit for each room.
  3. Clues: There's clues and hints for every challenge.
  4. Pay attention to the details: Even the smallest clue can be crucial to solving the challenges! There's also a lot of easter eggs hidden everywhere.
